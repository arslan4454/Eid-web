About Eid ul Adha

Eid ul Adha, the Festival of Sacrifice, commemorates the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim (AS) to sacrifice his son in obedience to God. It is a time of reflection, charity, devotion and community.

About Qurbani

Qurbani is the ritual animal sacrifice performed during Eid ul Adha as an act of worship and obedience to God. The meat is distributed among family, friends and the needy to promote sharing and compassion.
